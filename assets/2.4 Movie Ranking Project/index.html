The Best Three Movies According to Dhruv

My top three movies of all time

The Persuit of Happiness

It is the story of struggles faced by a man to actually support his family and work against the odds to be successful.

Phantom

It is the story how a person planned and executed the killing of the dangerous terrorist who did 26/11 Attack in Mumbai.

3 Idiots

It is a beautiful story of three friends in their college and teaches that we should work to make us capable rather than running after success.
